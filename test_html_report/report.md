# AI Agents: A Brief Overview

**Report Date:** December 2025 | **Sources:** 5

## Executive Summary

AI agents are autonomous systems that can perceive, reason, and act. This report examines their current state and impact on modern technology.

## Key Findings

### 1. Definition
AI agents are software systems that:
- Perceive their environment through sensors or data inputs
- Make decisions autonomously based on their goals
- Take actions to achieve objectives in dynamic environments
- Learn and adapt from their experiences

### 2. Market Growth
The AI agent market is projected to reach $50B by 2030 with compound annual growth of 23.5% [1].

![Market Growth](./images/chart.png)

### 3. Applications
- **Enterprise Automation:** Document processing, customer service, workflow management
- **Healthcare:** Diagnostic assistance, treatment planning, patient monitoring
- **Finance:** Algorithmic trading, fraud detection, portfolio optimization
- **Research:** Data analysis, hypothesis generation, experimental design

### 4. Challenges
- Transparency and interpretability of agent decisions
- Alignment with human values and safety constraints
- Data privacy and security concerns
- Integration with existing systems and workflows

## Technical Architecture

Successful AI agents typically consist of:
1. **Perception Module** - Input processing and environmental awareness
2. **Reasoning Engine** - Decision-making and planning
3. **Action Layer** - Executing decisions in the environment
4. **Learning System** - Continuous improvement through feedback

## Future Outlook

The next 2-3 years will likely see:
- Increased adoption of multi-agent systems
- Better interpretability tools and explainable AI
- Stronger regulatory frameworks
- Integration with edge computing and IoT devices
