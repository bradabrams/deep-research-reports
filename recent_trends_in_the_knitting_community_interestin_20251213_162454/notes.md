# Research Notes: Knitting Community Trends

## Session Log

### December 14, 2024 - Iteration 1

## Key Findings Summary

### 1. Color & Style Trends 2024
- **Blue** is THE color of 2024 (replacing hot pink from 2023)
- Often paired with white for colorwork
- Bold, bright colors and pastels both trending
- Nautical/preppy style influenced by Miu Miu fashion

### 2. Popular Garment Types
- **Cardigans** dominate - especially longer lengths
- Fair Isle/colorwork sweaters and vests
- Textured knits (Aran, brioche)
- Chunky cables and bold stripes

### 3. Trending Techniques
- **Fair Isle colorwork** - continuing strong from 2023
- **European Shoulder Construction** - shifted seam for Scandi designs
- **Textured knitting** - Aran, brioche for depth
- **Marling** - pairing contrasting colors for heathered effect
- **Fuzzy yarns + lace** - ethereal halo effects
- **Patchwork-inspired** - using leftover yarn, eco-friendly

### 4. Market & Demographics
- Global yarn market: $4.7B (2024) → $7.11B (2033)
- 71-98% of knitters are female
- Average yarn consumer age: 58 years (2024 survey)
- 45 million Americans know how to knit/crochet
- 74% of knitters use Ravelry

### 5. Pandemic Impact & Beyond
- COVID surge: Some retailers saw 646% increase
- Long-term trend shows decline from 2015 peak
- 56% buy more yarn online than before
- Pandemic shopping habits persist

### 6. Sustainability Movement
- 58% of demand driven by DIY/sustainable materials
- Growing recycled yarn options:
  - Knitting for Olive No Waste Wool (50% recycled)
  - Rowan Cotton Revive (regenerated cotton)
- Younger consumers (18-34) prioritizing eco-friendly

### 7. Economics & Pricing
- Cotton prices: 47.55¢/lb (2020) → 117.87¢/lb (2024)
- Merino wool up 56% since 2016
- Indie dyers make ~$2 profit per skein
- Tariffs may impact tools (most made in China)

### 8. Technology & Apps
- **KnitCompanion** - leading app (4.7/5 rating)
- **Stitch Fiddle** - free online chart maker
- **Knitrino** - emerging disruptive app
- 60%+ of hobbyists use online tutorials/apps

### 9. Size Inclusivity
- Standard now: patterns to 5X or 60" bust minimum
- Some designers offer up to 70"
- Resources: Victoria Marchant Knits spreadsheet
- Notable designers: Jessie Maed, Andrea Mowry, Tincanknits

### 10. Social Media & Community
- TikTok: Knitting as Spring 2024 trend
- #knitting: 970K+ TikTok posts, 23M+ Instagram posts
- #KnitTok community thriving
- Local Yarn Store Day 2024: April 27th

### 11. Craftivism & Activism
- Social Justice Sewing Academy
- Knitting Nannas Against Gas (Australia)
- Yarn Mission (Black Liberation focus)
- 2024 Charity Knitting Drive (TKGA)
